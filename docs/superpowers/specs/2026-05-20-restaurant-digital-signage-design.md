# Restaurant Digital Signage Slide — Design Spec

## Goal

A single professional slide to display on a restaurant digital screen, promoting UJ Studio Norge's photography and cinematography services to attract new clients.

## Format

- 1920×1080px landscape (standard digital signage)
- Single static slide

## Visual Design

**Palette:** Luxury Dark — black background (`#080808`), gold accents (`#c9a96e`), cream text (`#f5f0e8`)

**Layout:** Centered Elegance — all content centered on a pure black canvas

**Borders:** Double gold border with corner accent marks (inner + outer frame)

## Content

- **Studio name:** UJ Studio Norge — large serif gold, prominent at top
- **Headline:** "Moments Made" (cream) + "Eternal." (gold italic)
- **Divider:** Gold horizontal rule with diamond ornament
- **Services:** Photography · Cinematography · Digital Signage
- **Tagline:** "Capturing Norway's finest moments — weddings, fashion, and beyond."
- **CTA button:** "Book a Session" — gold outline
- **QR code:** Bottom center → links to `ujstudionorge.com/contact`
- **Contact:** `ujstudionorge.com/contact` + `@ujstudionorge`

## Fonts

- Headings: Serif (Playfair Display or equivalent)
- Body/labels: Sans-serif (DM Sans or equivalent), wide letter-spacing
